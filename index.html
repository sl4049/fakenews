About

Fake Me Out was developed by two graduate stduents from Teachers College, Columbia University to help news readers to become critical thinkers. Fake Me Out uses crowdsourcing to bring together different perspectives and help detect fake news. Be part of this movement and share your lastest headline!

How to Play

step1
Read the news article

step2
If you think the news isreal, choose real, if you think the news is fake, choose fake

step3
Flag the parts of the news that make you think it's real or fake

Headlines

As humans, we are always looking for ways to be part of a larger movement. Headlines helps your friends, family, and the world by providing crowdsourced news articles showing Fake Me Out results.
